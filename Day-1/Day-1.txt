Day-1: -

Print Modifiers - 
print("She said: "Hello" and then left.") #Does not print the word Hello
print('She said: "Hello" and then left.') #prints the word Hello
print("She said: \"Hello\" and then left.") #prints the word Hello

String Concatenation - 
print("Hello" + " " + "Aditya") --> Output - Hello Aditya
